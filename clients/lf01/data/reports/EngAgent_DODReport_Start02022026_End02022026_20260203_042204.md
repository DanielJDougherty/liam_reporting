## Taxonomy

**Main Categories:**
- `booking-completed`: Customer successfully completed appointment booking
- `booking-abandoned`: Customer started booking but did not complete
- `booking-transferred`: Booking attempt was transferred to human
- `transferred`: Call transferred without booking attempt
- `spam`: Spam, robocalls, wrong numbers
- `hangup`: Customer hung up (categorized by engagement level)
